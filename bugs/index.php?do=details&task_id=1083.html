I have 3 1920x1080 screens in a "triangle" so to speak. 2 are on the bottom next to each other with one on top in the middle. The screens are in the configuration that the first screen is the bottom left the second screen is top middle and the third screen is bottom right.

When ever I have focus on the third screen and launch applications they always show up on the second screen. I have a work around in place that on spawn the windows must be moved to the screen the mouse cursor/focus is on. However I would like to see a more permanent and less hacky solution.
