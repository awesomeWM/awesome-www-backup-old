Comment by Chris Ciulla (cciulla) - Friday, 21 March 2008, 11:54 GMT
Workaround: move statusbar stuff out of ~/.xinitrc into a new script, and wait until awesome is *completely* up (I shamelessly stole this script from calmar's awesome-status):

#!/bin/bash
sleep 1;
while ! pgrep awesome >/dev/null
do
echo "waiting for 'awesome-wm' to start"
sleep 2;
done

conky | awesome-client &
