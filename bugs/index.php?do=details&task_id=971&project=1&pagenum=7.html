Details

When switching between tags, and returning to the tag where matlab lives, all matlab windows are unfocused.
I cannot switch focus with mod+j/k and even clicking one of Matlab's windows will not focus it.
What works is to click one of Matlab's windows and then clicking another of Matlab's windows.
The second one will then get focus and things will work as usual until you switch away from the tag.
What also works is to maximize a window and then unmaximize it again...

See http://news.gmane.org/find-root.php?message_id=%3cCAOs%3dhRKqKz%3dcUi0bt1%2dZZbYBFZgRQwUByWZrykt5R6E235etNQ%40mail.gmail.com%3e for details.

In http://article.gmane.org/gmane.comp.window-managers.awesome/8437 another user mentions having the same problem with netbeans and other Java apps. He also mentions that the patch in #778 "seems to fix that".
